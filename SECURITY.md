# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The Aeternum Labs team appreciates your efforts to disclose your findings responsibly and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security issue, please contact us directly via email at **aeternumlabs@proton.me**. To help us understand and resolve the issue quickly, please include a detailed description of the vulnerability, steps to reproduce, potential impact, and any supporting proof-of-concept code.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Please DO NOT file a public issue on GitHub or disclose the vulnerability publicly in any way before it has been addressed by the Aeternum Labs core contributors.

## Scope

This security policy applies to all repositories and core infrastructure within the Aeternum ecosystem.

## Process

1. Submit your detailed report securely to **aeternumlabs@proton.me**.
2. The Aeternum Labs team will evaluate your report promptly and may request additional information or clarification.
3. Once validated, the team will prioritize working on a fix and coordinating the deployment release process.
4. After the patch is successfully deployed, a security advisory will be published, and your contribution will be formally acknowledged (if you wish to be credited).

Thank you for helping keep Aeternum and its users safe!
